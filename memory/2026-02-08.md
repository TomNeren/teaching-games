## Mammutprojekt → "Schicksalswege" Evolution
Massive progress on the generational decision-based history game:

### New Concept
- 4 family perspectives: Arbeiter, Bürger, Jüdisch, Adel
- 3 generations (1871-1914, 1914-1933, 1933-1945)
- 6 inheritable attributes: Bildung, Vermögen, Politik, Gesundheit, Ansehen, Solidarität (-100 to +100)
- Decision flags persist across generations (e.g. HAT_STREIK_TEILNAHME)
- Inheritance formula: Child = Parents/4 + Childhood_Experiences
