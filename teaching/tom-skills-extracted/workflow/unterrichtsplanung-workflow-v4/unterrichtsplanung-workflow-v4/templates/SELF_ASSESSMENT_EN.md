## Notes for Creation

1. **Content Questions (Part A):**
   - Directly related to learning objectives
   - Different cognitive levels (knowledge, comprehension, application)
   - Maximum 2-3 lines answer space per question

2. **Self-Assessment (Part B):**
   - Can be adapted to learning group
   - Smileys for visual self-rating

3. **Key Takeaways (Part C):**
   - Open questions for personal reflection
   - Space for honest feedback

4. **Next Steps (Part D):**
   - Promotes self-directed learning
   - Concrete action options provided

---

## Language Notes (B2+ Level)

For English lessons at B2+ level:
- Questions should use academic vocabulary where appropriate
- Expect full sentence answers
- Include at least one higher-order thinking question (analyze, evaluate, create)

---

2. What is the difference between "however" and "although"?
3. When do we use "furthermore" vs "moreover"?
4. Give an example of a formal alternative to "get".

### Reading (Text Analysis)
1. What is the main argument of the text?

3. How does the author support their claim?
